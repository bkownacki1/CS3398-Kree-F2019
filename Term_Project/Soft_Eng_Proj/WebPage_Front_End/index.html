Texas State Parking Apocolypse

Description of Project

This webpage was designed by five students of Texas State. Its purpose is to serve as a helpful tool for Texas State University Students, Faculty, and Staff to use to help them find the best parking lot or garage based on their unique, personal schedule. The way this tool was designed to be used is to take your schedule and your preferred mode transportation, then provide unique paths generated specifically for you to make your commute just a little bit easier. It would be a great idea to save the paths uniquely generated for you that way you don't have to return to the site every time you need to go to class.

Motivation of Project

So why did we make this? Well, of course, mostly for credit in our Software Engineering class. But we also made it to help people new to Texas State that way they can take full advantage of the parking opportunities and transportation paths available to them starting from day one, rather than having to figure it out themselves from day one like the rest of us. This tool was designed by Texas State Seniors and Full-Time Staff, so we believe that we have a firm grasp on the best available lots and paths.

Impact of Project

Our sincere hope is that this tool positively impacts everybody who regularly visits Texas State, no matter their reason. We hope that after using this tool, even if you've already spent years at Texas State, you will have been made aware of some rules, paths, and parking spots that you never knew existed. We know how stressful college can be, we hope to make parking into one less thing to worry about.
